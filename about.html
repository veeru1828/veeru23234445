KLE Society (Karnataka Lingayat Education Society), founded in 1916 in Belagavi, is one of India’s largest and most respected educational trusts. It manages over 310+ institutions including schools, colleges, medical institutions, engineering colleges, law colleges, and healthcare centers.

Established by seven visionary founders known as the “Saptarishis”, the society currently serves more than 145,000 students across India. Its focus areas include Education, Healthcare, Research, and Agriculture.

Key Institutions include:

• KLE Technological University, Hubballi (formerly BVB College of Engineering, est. 1947)
• KLE Academy of Higher Education & Research (KAHER)
• KLE Society’s Law College, Bangalore (est. 1975)

The society is led by Dr. Prabhakar Kore and continues to empower future generations through quality education and innovation.
